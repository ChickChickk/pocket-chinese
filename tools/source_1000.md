# 1,000 Practical Chinese Words

**Writing system:** Traditional Chinese  
**Regional focus:** Taiwan Mandarin  
**Format:** Chinese · Zhuyin · Pinyin · English · Example sentence · Translation · Stage

## Learning stages

- **Stage 1 (1–300):** immediate survival and basic conversation
- **Stage 2 (301–700):** independent daily life, study, and work
- **Stage 3 (701–1000):** natural conversation and more precise expression

> Notes: Meanings are intentionally concise. Measure words are included as core vocabulary. Some entries are common fixed expressions because they are more useful as complete spoken units.

## Suggested study method

Study 15–20 entries at a time. For nouns, learn the noun together with its usual measure word, such as **一本書**, **一張票**, or **一杯咖啡**. Say each entry aloud, create one personal sentence, and review it after 1 day, 3 days, 7 days, and 14 days.
